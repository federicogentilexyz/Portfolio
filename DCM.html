Federico Gentile
Works About Contacts
Year 2025
Type Audiovisual installation
Dimensions Structure (150x220x150cm)
Materials Plexiglass sphere, pvc pipes, black cloth, LCD display, cabling. A/V installation: real time generation made with touchdesigner.

Audio visual installation created with Ernesto Tommaso Campagnuolo for “Terreno Comune Festival”.

DCM is a memory chamber, an archive covered by a curtain behind which the visitor will find himself in front of a holographic display with rapid mnemonic impulses. Particles aggregate, get accelerated and dance in a swirl of flashing point bringing life to hidden artifacts of our subconscious in the form of clouds of holographic data.

The code that creates these representations was made with touchdesigner and is run with a generative audio track which triggers movement in the particles and renders different morphings of the point clouds. The hologram is created with a plexiglass sphere in which a tv reflects its image following the principle of the pepper’s ghost.
