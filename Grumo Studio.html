Ashtray//001 is the first product designed for Grumo Studio.

It’s a wheeltrown model that comes in three separable parts, designed to be shared. The layered structure allows for discreet storage of roaches and residue, keeping the surface clean and the form compact. Made in white stoneware, fired at ∆6 (1250°C) and finished with a dark green matte glaze.
