Federico Gentile
Works About Contacts
Year 2025-2026
Type Stage, visual and light design

Visual, stage, and lighting direction for Altera’s 2025–2026 season at Circolo Amelia in Milan.

The work focuses on developing a coherent spatial and aesthetic language that connects each event within the program. The design explores how light, reflective materials, and modular scenographic elements can reshape the venue’s atmosphere and respond dynamically to sound and performance.

Across the season, the role involves an ongoing dialogue with the artists and curators of Altera to translate the conceptual direction of each event into an immersive visual and spatial experience.

Presented at Circolo Amelia in Milan.
