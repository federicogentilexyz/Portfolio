Year 2023
Type Audio installation
Materials 24 channel surround soundsystem, ableton live, midi controller

24/’ventikwattro/ is a sound installation developed by IUAV students’ Ernesto Tommaso Campagnuolo, Federico Gentile and Nice De Blasio. The sound experience was developed during 2023, as an exam project for the sound design course, conducted by professor Riccardo Benassi.

Making use of synthesizers and drum machines the goal is to do research on the sounds that shake our soul and mixing them with everyday sounds to create an ambient heavily inspired by electronic tunes. From heavy bass pads to slick breakbeats “24” is a love letter to all deep thinkers and electronic dreamers who can’t live without their headphones on.
