Federico Gentile
Works About Contacts

Federico Gentile (2002) is a Italian/Lithuanian designer, visual artist and sound designer based between Venice and Berlin. He holds a BA in product and visual design at IUAV university of Venice and is currently pursuing a MA in visual arts and expanded cinema at the same institution. His work explores the emotional and aesthetic entanglements of digital culture, with a strong focus on the intersection between nature and technology.

He is the co-founder of Grumo Studio, an experimental design collective established in Venice with fellow designers, centered on product design through multidisciplinary research. Federico has also been the graphic designer and co-curator of Kiki Radio, a web radio operating between Venice and Paris, focused on sonic research, analyzing internet studies through the tool of experimental radio broadcasting.

His research has been featured in Medial Disorders vol. II by Inactual Magazine, and his work has been presented at renowned spaces such as Fondazione Bevilacqua La Masa and Fondazione Querini Stampalia in Venice. He is currently the resident stage and visual designer for Altéra, a hybrid music and performance series in Milan.

His broader practice moves across design, sound and installations.
