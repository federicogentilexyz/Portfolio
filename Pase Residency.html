Federico Gentile
Works About Contacts
Year 2024
Type Audio installation
Materials 24 channel surround soundsystem, magnetic tape, ableton live, midi controller

An audio installation presented at Pase Platform in Venice and curated by Francesco Fonassi. The exposition took place in two separate rooms. The first one showcased an audio track composed with different samples, the use of granular synthesis and spatial audio controlled by Pase’s high-tech soundsystem.

The observer could than use a midi controller to move the sound objects across space to create distinctive echoes, reverbs, and sound effects, manipulating the track based on personal taste. In the second room a web of magnetic tape runs through a structure showcasing a “physical” version of the sounds that could’ve been heard in the first room.

The tape than runs through effect pedals adding an analog glaze to digitally crafted samples.

Pase is a cultural organization that explores, thinks of and takes on new art languages. It began its journey in 2011, as it started to elaborate moments of formation , discussion and performance that are connected to contemporary music. Through the years, Pase has had the possibility to work alongside artists of various backgrounds, implicated in the worlds of art and contemporary music, by way of an involvement in the processes of curatorship, production and organization

Part of a residence program organized by DIY Collective and IUAV University of Venice at Pase Platform.
